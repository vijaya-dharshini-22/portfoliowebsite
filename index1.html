About Me

Through this experience, I realized the importance of proactive measures in protecting online data and preventing cybercrimes. I am enthusiastic about continuing to expand my knowledge in these areas and hope to contribute to the development of more advanced and secure systems in the future. With my academic background and hands-on project experience, I am committed to making a meaningful impact in the field of IT, particularly in cybersecurity.
